Data Deletion

Last updated: June 28, 2026

BlinkLit is built so that your reading data stays with you. Most of it never reaches us at all. This page explains exactly what data exists, where it lives, and how to delete each kind — including the only piece we process on a server: the email tied to your Pro license.

1. Data on your device

Your library, reading progress, bookmarks, settings and appearance preferences are stored locally on your device. To delete all of it, uninstall the app — this removes every locally stored item immediately.

2. Google Drive sync data

If you turned on optional cloud sync, your data lives in your own Google Drive (under the drive.file scope, so only BlinkLit's own files). To delete it:

  • Delete the BlinkLit files/folder from your Google Drive, and/or
  • Revoke BlinkLit's access from your Google Account's connected-apps settings.

3. Your Pro license email (the only server-side data)

When you buy BlinkLit Pro, we store your email address on our licensing backend solely to issue and validate your license. This is the only personal data we hold. To have it permanently deleted:

  • Email blinklitofficial@gmail.com from the address tied to your license, with the subject "Data deletion request".
  • We will delete your email and associated license record within 30 days and confirm by reply.

Note: deleting your license email also deactivates the Pro license linked to it. Any purchase records required for tax or accounting are held by Google Play under its own policies, not by us.

4. Questions

For anything about your data, contact blinklitofficial@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
